How to Use

Follow these simple steps to use the BMI prediction tool:

  1. Upload or capture clear facial image of the person whose BMI you want to predict.
  2. Click the "Predict BMI" button to initiate the prediction process.
  3. Review the provided BMI prediction.
  4. Before going for new prediction press clear button.
  5. The pedicted BMI is not 100% accurate.

Basic understanding of BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure that helps assess an individual's body weight in relation to their height. It is commonly used as a simple and quick screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight status categories. BMI is calculated using the following formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m)2

BMI categories:

  • BMI less than 18.5 --> Malnourished
  • BMI between 18.5 to 24.5 --> Normal
  • BMI more than 24.5 --> Obese
